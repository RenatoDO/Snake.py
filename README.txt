Simple Snake game made with tkinter for GUI and mixer to add sound

You can change dificulty by changing SPEED from 150 to a lower number like 100 to make 
the snake faster and SPACE_SIZE to 25 to make the snake smaller (Food size is related to 
SPACE_SIZE).

To play press GamesStart
W or Up arrow to move up
S to Down arrow move down
A to Left arrow move left
D to Right arrow move right
